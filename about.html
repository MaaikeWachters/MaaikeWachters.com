About me

Education
I have a Bachelor's degree in Cultural Studies as well as in History, and a Master's degree in Intercultural Communication, so I guess I was supposed to be a struggling artist or a teacher. Instead I spent a few years sending students abroad, taught English in China, and then became a communication professional.

Work experience
I'm an allround communication professional. I am an excellent copywriter and storyteller, video editor, graphic designer and pubquiz maker & quiz master. I've worked in higher education for over 10 years now, first in internationalisation and then communication. Now I'd like to get a job in the real world by becoming a front end developer.

Travel & languages
I have lived in the United States, Australia, Finland and China. I've always thrived in international environments. My mother tongue is Dutch, but I speak English at a native level. I speak a bit of French and Spanish, and you wil often find me pretending to speak German. I also know enough Mandarin Chinese to get into discussions about directions with taxi drivers in Beijing.

Free time
In my free time I like to write letters, read books, draw, cook Chinese & Korean food and take long walks & bike rides. I love striped socks, and according to the internet that is a sign of intelligence, so there you go.
